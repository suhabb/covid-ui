You are required to provide a simple implementation of a PlayerEventHandler. You may use Java version 11 or above.

The recommended time to complete this exercise should be between forty-five minutes and one hour.

Expected event types

Started : published when at beginning of streaming
Stopped : published when viewing stopped
Acceptance Criteria

Bookmarking service may return multiple bookmarks with different stream positions for a given user/contentId
In case of duplicate bookmarks, newer bookmarks supersede older ones.
Ignore bookmarks older than 30 days.
Any other events should be ignored.
